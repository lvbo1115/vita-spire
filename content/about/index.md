VitaSpire is a trusted manufacturer of bio-industrial products, specializing in three areas of biomaterial applications: biomedical and healthcare materials, human nutrition, and advanced industrial and electronic materials. Our products are widely used in various fields of industrial production, food and health product development, and advanced materials research.

## Biomedical & Healthcare Materials

In the field of biomedical and healthcare materials, we focus on the production of human bone-compatible materials such as hydroxyapatite and tricalcium phosphate, providing professional raw material support for bone repair, dental restoration, and the production of biocompatible scaffolds for bioactive coatings. We have also developed high-quality medical aesthetic implant materials, offering more possibilities for the innovative development of the medical aesthetics industry. In home care, we develop specialized raw materials for toothpaste formulations for personal dental care. Our full range of products provides comprehensive support for the biomedical and healthcare industry.

## Human Nutrition Solutions

In the field of human nutrition, we focus on the extraction of natural, high-quality raw materials. Derived from nature, giving back to humanity. We select high-quality plant raw materials and utilize advanced extraction processes to maximize the retention of active ingredients, ensuring the purity, safety, and stability of our raw materials. Our products include ginseng, ginkgo, green tea, soybeans, and grape seeds, supporting diverse health needs such as antioxidant protection, immune enhancement, and metabolic improvement, helping you create more competitive health products.

## Advanced Industrial & Electronic Materials

In the field of advanced industrial and electronic materials, our main products include graphene, carbon nanotubes, and fluorinated electronic fluids. These products provide high-performance raw material support for the research and development of new materials and the manufacturing of electronic components.
